01

Product Security

Helping product companies achieve EU Cyber Resilience Act (CRA) and IEC 62443 compliance through practical, hands-on implementation of secure development practices.

CRA Deadline: December 2027

Full compliance required. Reporting obligations start September 2026. Fines up to €15 million or 2.5% of global turnover.

CRA Readiness Assessment

Evaluate your product against EU Cyber Resilience Act requirements. Get a clear gap analysis and compliance roadmap.

SDL Assessment

Evaluate your development process against IEC 62443-4-1. Understand your maturity level and certification readiness.

Threat Modeling Workshop

Hands-on workshop with your team to identify security threats and define mitigations using STRIDE methodology.

Secure Architecture Review

Deep-dive technical review of product architecture covering authentication, cryptography, secure boot, and update mechanisms.

SDL Implementation Support

Hands-on help implementing secure development practices: coding standards, security testing, SBOM, vulnerability management.

Developer Security Training

Hands-on workshop covering secure coding, OWASP Top 10, threat modeling basics, and CRA/IEC 62443 awareness.
